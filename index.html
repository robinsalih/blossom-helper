Blossom word placer

This helps you find the optimal solution for the blossom puzzle. Enter in the centre letter and the petals. Then enter the words you have found and it will find the best way to allocate them to the petals. It checks that words are valid (i.e. contains the centre letter etc.), but not that they are in the dictionary. It will calculate the score you will get. Enter your words, and as soon as you have 12 or more words you can press the go button!
